Privacy Policy

We at Health & Wellness Blog value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

When you use our website, we collect the following types of information:

Account Information: When you register, we collect your name and email address to create and manage your account.

Contact Information: When you reach out to our specialists through the contact form, we collect your name, email, and message content.

Comments and Interactions: If you comment on blog posts or interact with our content, we store this information to manage our community.

Usage Data: We collect anonymous data about how you use our website to improve our services.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

- To provide personalized health and wellness guidance from our specialists

- To respond to your questions and inquiries

- To improve our content and services based on user feedback

- To send you updates about new articles and content (only with your consent)

- To ensure the security and proper functioning of our website

Your Health Information

While our blog provides health and wellness information, we are not a medical facility and do not collect or store medical records. Any health-related information you share through our contact form is used only to provide general guidance and is treated with strict confidentiality.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

- Access your personal information that we hold

- Request correction of any inaccurate information

- Request deletion of your information

- Opt-out of marketing communications

- Request a copy of your data

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze website traffic. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. We only use essential cookies and analytics tools that help us improve our services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the date below.

Contact Us About Privacy

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please reach out through our Contact page. We're here to address any concerns you may have about your privacy.
